Beginning Of Bagan

Bagan began as a small settlement and become important around 849 CE.Under King Anawrahta in the 11th century, it grew into a powerful kingdom and a center of Therawada Buddhism. His rule marked the start of bagan's golden age, with thousands of temples built-many of which still stand today.
